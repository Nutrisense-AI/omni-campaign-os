# OmniCampaign: Your AI Marketing Team in a Box - Video Script

## Scene 1: Hook (0-5 seconds)

**Visual:** Fast-paced montage of small business owners looking stressed, juggling multiple marketing tasks (social media, ads, landing pages, emails).

**Audio:** Upbeat, slightly frantic music. Sound of keyboard clicks, phone notifications.

**Voiceover (Energetic, friendly):** "Tired of juggling a million marketing tasks? Wish you had an entire marketing team at your fingertips?"

## Scene 2: The Problem (5-10 seconds)

**Visual:** A single, overwhelmed business owner looking at a complex spreadsheet or a blank social media post.

**Audio:** Music softens slightly, a sigh sound effect.

**Voiceover:** "Creating engaging content, running ads, building landing pages, sending emails... it's a full-time job. And it's taking you away from what you do best."

## Scene 3: Introducing OmniCampaign (10-15 seconds)

**Visual:** Smooth transition to the OmniCampaign dashboard. Highlight the 'Magic Prompt' input field.

**Audio:** Uplifting, confident music begins.

**Voiceover:** "Introducing OmniCampaign – your AI marketing team in a box. Just tell us what you need..."

## Scene 4: The Magic Prompt (15-20 seconds)

**Visual:** User types a simple prompt into OmniCampaign: "Launch a new coffee shop, targeting young professionals, 20% off first order."

**Audio:** Satisfying keyboard sounds, a 'ding' as the prompt is submitted.

**Voiceover:** "...and watch the magic happen."

## Scene 5: AI Generation in Action (20-35 seconds)

**Visual:** Rapid-fire montage showcasing OmniCampaign generating assets:
- Claude writing Instagram captions, Facebook ad copy.
- gpt-image-1 creating a stunning image for the coffee shop.
- ElevenLabs generating a natural-sounding voiceover.
- Shotstack rendering a dynamic vertical video for TikTok.
- A beautiful landing page being built in real-time.

**Audio:** Fast-paced, futuristic sound effects, subtle AI whirs.

**Voiceover:** "OmniCampaign instantly generates everything you need: captivating social media posts, compelling ad copy, eye-catching images, engaging TikTok videos with voiceovers, and even a high-converting landing page – all from a single prompt!"

## Scene 6: The Command Center (35-45 seconds)

**Visual:** Pan across the OmniCampaign dashboard, highlighting:
- **Campaign Workspace:** Tabs for Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Landing Page with previews and download buttons.
- **AI Co-pilot:** User types "make the tone more playful" and the copy updates.
- **CRM Kanban:** Dragging a lead from 'New' to 'Contacted'.
- **Unified Inbox:** A new message appearing from a website visitor.
- **Email Marketing:** Composing a broadcast email.

**Audio:** Smooth, efficient sounds. A gentle 'whoosh' as assets update.

**Voiceover:** "Manage it all from one intuitive command center. Tweak your content with the AI co-pilot, track leads in your CRM, respond to messages in your unified inbox, and send email broadcasts to your subscribers. OmniCampaign puts you in control."

## Scene 7: Benefits & Call to Action (45-60 seconds)

**Visual:** Happy business owner checking their phone, seeing increased engagement/sales. OmniCampaign logo prominently displayed.

**Audio:** Upbeat, triumphant music.

**Voiceover:** "Stop wasting time and money on fragmented marketing tools. OmniCampaign streamlines your workflow, boosts your results, and frees you up to focus on your business. Get started today and transform your marketing with AI!"

**Text Overlay:**
"OmniCampaign: Your AI Marketing Team in a Box"
"Visit OmniCampaign.com to get started!"
